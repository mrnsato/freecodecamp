Homemade Bread

Ingredients

  • 1kg of wheat flour
  • 600ml of water
  • 60ml of oil
  • 4 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 package of biological yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Start by mixing the oil, sugar, salt and yeast.
  2. Then add all the wheat flour and half the water and mix.
  3. If you need more water, add it until you reach the desired consistency. After that, we will knead the dough for at least 15 minutes.
  4. After kneading, cover it with a cloth and let it rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Then divide it into two parts and use your fingers to open the dough, open it well and then roll it.
  6. Place the dough in the greased molds, brush with egg yolks to add color and bake in a preheated oven at 180C for 30 minutes.
